    About Me

    Who I am

    I’m a PhD student at the Brain and Spine Institute (ICM), inside the Pitié-Salpêtrière Hospital in Paris. I’m working under the supervision of Fabrizio De Vico Fallani on brain connectivity which lays at the intersect between three main fields: neuroscience, signal processing and, of course, complex systems science.

    What I do

    Until now, I’ve been mainly working on building multilayer brain networks where both the nodes, that correspond to brain regions, and the edges, that link them, can be estimated in many different ways.
